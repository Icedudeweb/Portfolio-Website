Virginia City

Here my first step was deciding what I liked about both high-quality source images. I chose the movie logo from the image on the left, but decided to use it on the image on the right.

The next step was isolating and vectoring the logo. Once vectored I could manipulate it to adjust color, add strokes, and create drop shadows.

As with Dallas, I then traced the key graphics and cleaned/color corrected them. Both the logo trace and the graphics trace gave me "clean" elements with which to get creative!

For the final image, I decided to give the background a golden yellow hue (the movie centers around the recovery of Confederate gold), and for contrast gave the logo a deep red. Recreating the tagline, adding actor credits to match the poster on the right, and adding the composer credits finishes the image (click to enlarge).
