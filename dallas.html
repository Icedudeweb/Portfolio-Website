Dallas

My first step was finding a suitably large enough image to work with. It's got a little wear but is generally pretty clean.

After evaluating the color scheme, the second step was deciding to modestly update the overall look by creating a new blue gradient background image.

I then traced all the key graphics (the humans and the mountains) in Photoshop and carefully cleaned/color corrected them. To really make the whole thing "pop" beyond that process, though, I decided to recreate the movie logo.

Once this was completed and placed on the new layout, I recreated the tagline and custom created composer credits to finish the image (click to enlarge).
